    Everyone has limitations and the work that is done is a little less so the society is very big then the question is how to help as many people as possible and how this work will inspire the pictures.

    About us

    The InterBeing Foundation is a non-governmental organization with the vision of “closing the gap between health, education, the environment, agriculture, and society.” “InterBeing” is the term coined by Thich Nhath Hanh, which is a combination of “Togetherness” and “Connection”. That is why we believe in the inter-existence of all beings here at the InterBeing Foundation. We support and respect the rights of all living beings on this earth. Equal rights for all living beings (existence). We also welcome everyone, without any discrimination based on religion, region, gender, skin color, or race. We work for the welfare of people at all borders. Health, the environment, education, agriculture, and peace are the main concerns of the world today and most of our projects will be based on these themes. Our team also has a lot of practical experience in these areas, they have worked in towns and urban areas, have had contact with many people, and have learned their basic questions about health and hygiene, unequal availability of pure water, affected agriculture with very little rainfall, facilities updated educational programs, etc. It is a great team that can work for the good of society. We are committed to the idea of ”giving back to society”.

    What We do

    Health

    Blood and Organ Donation: Use an extended perspective of planned behavioral theory to identify similarities and differences between individual motivations for donations. Interbeing is a way to encourage people, teens, and younger generations to participate, donate and inspire others.

    Education

    Education is the power to cultivate the human mind. Education is what makes a person fearless, teaches him the lesson of unity, makes him aware of his rights, and inspires him to struggle for his rights. Education is a movement. If it does not fulfill its objectives, it is useless.
    True education cradles humanity, generates sources of livelihood, imparts wisdom, and imbues us with egalitarianism. It is a dynamic force in the life of an individual and influences his physical, mental, social, emotional, ethical, creative, and innovative development. It helps the individual in undergoing appropriate needed experiences and in organizing these experiences into meaning, for his life activities.

    Environment

    The environment can be defined as a sum total of all the living and non-living beings & elements. And their effects on humans and each of them. The environment consists of factors and conditions in the surroundings which may have an impact on the development, action, or/and survival of each and every organism or group of organisms on earth.
    Healthy ecosystems clean our water, purify our air, maintain our soil, regulate the climate, recycle nutrients and provide us with well-balanced food. It provides raw materials and resources for medicines and other purposes. It is at the foundation of all Civilization, Empires, Rulers and to sustain our modern economies. The environment has emerged as an important issue because every society is faced economic crises which ultimately reach the condition of different wars and at last result in unending social problems. It matters because Earth is the only home that we humans have, and it provides us air, food and fulfilled our other needs.

    Agriculture

    Agriculture has been associated with the production of essential food crops and other allied products. Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economic system. In addition, it provides food and raw material to other Industries, similarly, it provides the largest employment opportunities in the country. The importance of proper agricultural knowledge, Technologies, and Technics help to improve biodiversity encompass socio-cultural, economic, and environmental elements. All domesticated animals and crops result from proper and well-balanced human management of biodiversity, which is constantly under pressure and facing new challenges to maintain and increase productivity under constantly varying conditions due to climate change & Global warming.

    Social Divides

    The soul of Human freedom lies in his right to live equally with other human beings without discriminating him on the basis of caste, the color of skin, religion, race, gender, economic, etc. Interbeing act as a bridge between these inequalities which lies in the Irony of the word Equality for having and have not.

    Scientific Temper

    Scientific temperament is important for: Bringing forth a progressive society that is free of superstitions and irrational practices. Developing the nation in all spheres (political, economic, and social) Promoting tolerance among people for differing thoughts and ideas.
